My name's Rich(ard) Bingham. I research and write about digital cultures.

At the moment I have a few projects on the go:

  1. De-generate!

    In which I argue against using generational identities like 'millennial' to understand societies today. An extension of my doctoral research.

  2. Recursions

    A wide-ranging analysis of the videogame The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask. Examining this game from numerous angles, I construct a new approach to writing about digital cultural texts.

  3. On Realistics

    In which I uncover the 'realistic' as a complex, ever-changing 'aesthetic category'. An alternative cultural history to the existing literature on 'realism' in the arts.
